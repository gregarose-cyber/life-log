Life Log

Your personal journal, powered by AI.

About

Life Log is a private journaling app that helps you capture moments, memories, and thoughts. With AI-generated titles and tags, your entries are automatically organized so you can focus on writing.

Features

  • Quick capture for fast thoughts on the go
  • AI-generated entry titles and tags
  • Photo attachments
  • Location tagging
  • Voice-to-text entry
  • Face ID / Touch ID lock
  • Templates for meals, movies, places, and more

Privacy Policy

Life Log takes your privacy seriously.

  • Your journal entries are stored securely in your personal account and are never shared with third parties.
  • Entry content is sent to Anthropic's Claude API solely to generate titles and tag suggestions. This data is not stored or used for training.
  • Location data is only used to tag entries and is never sold or shared.
  • We do not display ads or sell your data.
